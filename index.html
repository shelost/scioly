Ithaca Science Olympiad

Science Olympiad is the place to be for science lovers!

We train to compete in a wide range of events, from Astronomy to Forensics to building machines. An award-winning club, Science Olympiad allows students who are interested in the latest in science to take part in stimulating and team-building competitions.

We meet from 3:30-4:30 after school on Mondays in Mr. Fellman's room (E31) and Wednesdays in the library.
